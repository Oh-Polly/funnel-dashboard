Likely drivers
Hypotheses to validate with marketing — directional (from GA4, which has a UK consent-mode gap), not proven causes:
  • Free-traffic erosion (lead). Organic search and direct/brand demand softening; paid has scaled but isn't fully offsetting it.
  • Seasonality. Traffic peaked in early May and has eased into summer — partly the normal spring→summer cycle.
  • Weaker top-of-funnel reach. Email and organic social are softer, pulling in fewer new visitors.
  • Paid efficiency. Paid up while total is down — worth checking it's incremental, not replacing organic at a cost.
Shopify (Oh Polly UK online store). Visitors = unique online-store visitors. Current part-week excluded so every week is settled. Web only — excludes the Tapcart app.
